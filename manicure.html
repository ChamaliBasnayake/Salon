Pre-care and After-care Advice and Guidance

Manicures:

  • Make sure you reserve enough time for after the manicure, to allow your nail polish to dry before you leave the salon.
  • We recommend using hand and cuticle cream everyday.
  • If you want to change the polish yourself between visits, remember to use a clear polish or base coat to prevent discolouring the nail.
  • Apply a top coat to protect, prolong and help prevent the chipping of the nail polish.

Pedicures:

  • Make sure you reserve enough time for after the pedicure, to allow the nail polish to dry before you leave the salon.
  • We recommend that you bring flip flops with you, to protect from smudging or damage of the polish.
  • Where possible, use a pumice stone in between appointments to avoid the build up of hard, dead skin.
  • If you want to change the polish yourself between visits, remember to use a clear polish or base coat to prevent the discoloration of the nail.
  • To maintain hydrated and soft feet, moisturise them daily.
  • To avoid dryness, exfoliate weekly.

Nail-on-Nail information

On top of their many purposes and services, the nails protect and sustain the tissues in our fingers and toes. They are made up of hardened skin cells consisting mainly of the protein keratin, which is also found in skin and hair.

There are many nail disorders that are a result of improper and poor nail care. It is important to maintain good nail-care habits; Dermatologists recommend that nails are kept clean and dry in order to maintain healthy nails. The purpose of this is to keep bacteria from developing under the nail. Doctors will often look at nails to detect a connecting health condition such as liver or kidney diseases, anaemia, diabetes and even heart disease as they can portray serious health problems.

How can I uphold healthy nails?

Professional manicures or pedicures performed on a regular basis can be very helpful in maintaining the overall health of the nail. Manicures and pedicures not only enhance the beauty of your hands and feet, but they also assist in the prevention of hang nails, nail damage and reduce the chance of developing fragile tips, splits and cuts on the nails. Additionally, the massages provided with our Essential & Signature treatments may help improve the skins health by enhancing the blood circulation to the area; this discourages wrinkles developing on your hands and feet.
